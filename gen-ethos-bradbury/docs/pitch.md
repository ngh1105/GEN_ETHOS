# GEN-ETHOS Pitch Pack

## One-Line Hero

GEN-ETHOS turns corporate claims into staked commitments that can be publicly audited and economically enforced.

## 30-Second Pitch

Companies publish sustainability and compliance claims every day, but today those claims are mostly self-reported, slow to verify, and consequence-free. GEN-ETHOS changes that. A company registers a claim, stakes escrow behind it, and submits evidence for audit. GenLayer validators compare independent public sources, produce a consensus verdict, and the protocol updates both reputation and escrow outcome. We start with greenwashing and emissions claims, but the same system can verify any corporate disclosure where trust matters.

## 60-Second Pitch

The problem is simple: corporate claims are easy to publish and hard to verify. That gap creates greenwashing, weak accountability, and low public trust. It is getting worse because AI makes polished claims even easier to generate.

GEN-ETHOS is a verification and enforcement layer for corporate disclosures. Instead of treating a claim like marketing, we treat it like a commitment. A company registers a target, stakes escrow, and requests an audit using public evidence sources. GenLayer validators independently analyze those sources and reach consensus on a verdict such as compliant, minor violation, violation, or inconclusive.

That verdict is not just informational. It changes the company state. Compliant outcomes unlock value. Violations slash escrow and reduce score. Every audit is visible in a public history.

We start with ESG and emissions claims because greenwashing is a clear and urgent wedge, but the platform can extend to supply chain claims, vendor compliance, impact reporting, and other trust-sensitive disclosures.

## 3-Minute Demo Script

### Step 1: Setup

Open the app and say:

"This company is about to make a public sustainability commitment. In GEN-ETHOS, a claim is not just text. It becomes a stateful on-chain commitment."

### Step 2: Register Company

Go to onboarding and register a company with a target reduction.

Say:

"First, the company registers its target. This creates a public identity and baseline for future audits."

### Step 3: Deposit Escrow

Deposit escrow for the same company.

Say:

"Next, the company stakes value behind the claim. This is important because now the claim has economic accountability."

### Step 4: Request Audit

Open the audit engine and submit a request using your stable preset data.

Say:

"Now we trigger an audit using public evidence sources. GenLayer validators independently reason over the evidence and reach a consensus verdict."

### Step 5: Show Verdict

Wait for the result card and transaction status.

Say:

"The output is not just a score. The protocol gives a verdict that can drive consequences: compliant, minor violation, violation, or inconclusive if evidence is weak."

### Step 6: Show Consequence

Move to Explorer and show score, escrow balance, unlocked amount, and audit history.

Say:

"Here is the important part: the verdict updates protocol state. Good behavior can unlock value. Violations can slash escrow and reduce reputation. The audit history is public."

### Step 7: Close

End with:

"So GEN-ETHOS is not an ESG dashboard. It is a verification and enforcement layer for corporate claims, starting with sustainability disclosures."

## Demo Rules

- Use one clean flow only
- Use stable preset data only
- Prefer one `INCONCLUSIVE` example and one `VIOLATION` example
- Do not improvise new URLs live
- Keep the demo under 3 minutes
- Always end on consequence, not on form submission
